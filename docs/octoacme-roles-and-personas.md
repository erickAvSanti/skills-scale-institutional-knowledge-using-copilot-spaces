# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
The QA Lead coordinates quality assurance activities across the project, ensuring that testing strategies are comprehensive and acceptance criteria are clearly defined and validated.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test strategies for features and releases
- Ensure acceptance criteria are testable and clearly documented
- Coordinate with developers to establish entry/exit criteria for testing phases
- Lead bug triage and collaborate with Product Manager and Developers on prioritizing fixes
- Verify readiness for release by validating critical flows and regression tests
- Maintain test documentation and quality metrics

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality releases with minimal post-deployment defects
- Establish clear, repeatable testing processes
- Provide visibility into quality metrics and test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups to report on testing progress and blockers
- Sprint planning to review acceptance criteria and testability
- Bug triage meetings and release readiness reviews
- Post-release retrospectives to identify quality improvements

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with Developers to ensure code is testable and meets acceptance criteria
- Collaborates with Product Manager to clarify requirements and validate user scenarios
- Coordinates with Project Manager on test timelines and release gates
- Partners with Developers on fixing critical bugs and release blockers

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
The UX Designer owns the user experience design process, creating intuitive interfaces that meet user needs and business goals through research, wireframes, prototypes, and iterative design.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions
- Create wireframes, user flows, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Ensure designs align with accessibility standards and brand guidelines
- Collaborate with stakeholders and end users to validate design solutions
- Partner with developers to ensure design feasibility and fidelity during implementation
- Iterate on designs based on user feedback and metrics

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered designs that improve usability and satisfaction
- Reduce friction in user workflows and increase task completion rates
- Maintain design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager and stakeholders
- Handoff sessions with Developers to explain design specs and interactions
- User research readouts and usability test findings
- Iteration planning to prioritize design refinements

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates closely with Product Manager to translate requirements into intuitive solutions
- Works with Developers during implementation to ensure design fidelity
- Partners with QA Lead to define user scenarios for testing
- Presents to Sponsor and stakeholders for design approvals and feedback

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
The Business Analyst bridges the gap between business needs and technical solutions, translating requirements into clear, actionable specifications that guide development and ensure alignment with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Perform workflow analysis and process mapping
- Break down complex requirements into detailed user stories with clear acceptance criteria
- Facilitate backlog grooming sessions to refine and prioritize work
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business requirements
- Identify opportunities for process improvement and efficiency gains

### Goals
- Ensure technical solutions align with business objectives
- Reduce ambiguity in requirements and acceptance criteria
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction and business value delivery

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering sessions with business stakeholders
- Backlog refinement meetings with Product Manager and Project Manager
- Clarification discussions with Developers about implementation details
- Status updates and demos to validate progress against business needs

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with Project Manager and Product Manager for backlog grooming and scope alignment
- Works with Developers to clarify requirements and answer technical questions
- Collaborates with UX Designer to ensure designs meet business workflows
- Communicates with stakeholders to validate requirements and gather feedback

---

## Sponsor

### Role Summary
The Sponsor is an executive-level champion who provides strategic direction, secures resources, removes organizational roadblocks, and approves major decisions to ensure project success and alignment with business strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Provide strategic vision and business justification for the project
- Secure funding, resources, and executive support
- Approve major scope changes, budget adjustments, and timeline shifts
- Remove organizational and cross-functional roadblocks
- Review progress at key milestones and decision gates
- Ensure alignment with broader business objectives and priorities
- Make final decisions on escalated issues and trade-offs

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers expected business value and ROI
- Maintain executive visibility and support for the initiative
- Enable the team to succeed by removing barriers and securing resources

### Typical Communication
- Monthly or milestone-based status reviews with Project Manager
- Escalation discussions for critical blockers or decisions
- Executive steering committee updates
- Approval gates for major deliverables or phase transitions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with Project Manager for status updates and escalations
- Partners with Product Manager to validate business outcomes and priorities
- Provides direction and decision-making for unresolved cross-team issues
- Engages periodically with the full team at kickoffs, demos, and retrospectives
